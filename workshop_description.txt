[h1]Space POI Mover[/h1]

Move any space POI to wherever you want on the starmap.

[b]How to Use[/b]
[olist]
[*]Open Starmap (M key)
[*]Click a POI
[*]Click "Move This" or "Move All"
[*]Click destination
[/olist]

[b]Features[/b]
[list]
[*]Move harvestable POIs & artifact POIs
[*]Move single or all POIs on a hex
[*]Visual feedback (green=valid, red=invalid)
[/list]

Requires Spaced Out! DLC

[hr]

[h1]太空POI移动器[/h1]

把太空POI移动到星图上任意位置。

[b]使用方法[/b]
[olist]
[*]打开星图 (M键)
[*]点击POI
[*]点击"Move This 移动选中"或"Move All 移动全部"
[*]点击目标位置
[/olist]

[b]功能[/b]
[list]
[*]移动资源POI和文物POI
[*]单独或批量移动
[*]可视化反馈 (绿色=有效，红色=无效)
[/list]

需要眼冒金星DLC
