# Space POI Mover / 太空POI移动器

## English

### Description
Freely move Space Points of Interest (POIs) around the starmap! Tired of that resource asteroid being too far from your colony? Just pick it up and move it closer!

### Features
- **Move Any Space POI**: Includes harvestable POIs, artifact POIs, and more
- **Intuitive UI**: A side panel shows all movable POIs in your cluster
- **Visual Feedback**: Valid destination hexes highlight in green, invalid ones in red
- **Easy to Use**: Select a POI → Click Move → Click destination

### How to Use
1. Open the Starmap (press M or click the starmap button)
2. Look for the "Space POI Mover" panel on the left side
3. Click on a POI from the list to select it
4. Click the "Move" button
5. Click on any valid hex on the starmap to move the POI there
6. Press ESC or click "Cancel" to cancel the move

### Limitations
- Cannot move POIs onto asteroid hexes
- Cannot stack multiple POIs on the same hex
- POI locations are saved with your game save

### Compatibility
- Works with Spaced Out! DLC
- Compatible with most other mods

## 中文

### 简介
自由移动太空兴趣点（POI）！厌倦了资源小行星离你的殖民地太远？只需把它拿起来移到更近的地方！

### 功能特点
- **移动任何太空POI**：包括可采集POI、文物POI等
- **直观的用户界面**：侧边面板显示星图中所有可移动的POI
- **视觉反馈**：有效目标格显示为绿色，无效格显示为红色
- **操作简单**：选择POI → 点击移动 → 点击目标位置

### 使用方法
1. 打开星图（按M键或点击星图按钮）
2. 在左侧找到"Space POI Mover"面板
3. 从列表中点击选择一个POI
4. 点击"Move"按钮
5. 点击星图上任意有效的六角格来移动POI
6. 按ESC键或点击"Cancel"取消移动

### 限制
- 无法将POI移动到小行星所在的格子
- 无法在同一格子上堆叠多个POI
- POI位置会随游戏存档保存

### 兼容性
- 支持眼冒金星DLC
- 与大多数其他MOD兼容
